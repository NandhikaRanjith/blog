must visit places

golden gate park

i visited in september 2021

Golden gate park has a ferris wheel, de young museum, the california academy of sciences, and the japanese tea garden, which are major spots in sfo. The california academy of sciences has a planetarium and a tropical place, and a new shark exibit, and the de young museum has amazing art. The ferris wheel is slow but fun a the top. Golden gate park also has the san frnacisco botanical garden, where you can see plants from all around the world and have a lot of fun. The japanese tea garden has nice scenery too.



New york

i visited in august 2019

New York City, Manhattan, Ellis Island and Niagara Falls were the top attractions in this state. New York City has the Empire State building and Town Square, wheich as some really cool shops and entertainment. Ellis Island has the statue of liberty, which holds a lot of historic importance. Manhattan is another major city in New York, and Niagara Falls consists of 3 waterfalls that are in the US-Canada border.All of them were really fun and I had a great time there.
